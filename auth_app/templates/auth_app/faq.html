Frequently Asked Questions

What knowledge do I need to complete the challenge?
Programming (any lenguage o paradigm) and WebSockets.
May I use any language?
Yes. You can use any programming language that a WebScockets client can manage.
Do I have to implement all game rules and piece movements?

No. You only have to apply the movements that you consider necessary.
Keep in mind that as many features you implement, you will increase your chances of getting better results in tournaments.
However, we want you to focus on the quality of the code.

Can I play more than one match at the same time?
Yes. During a tournament, players can be playing in more than one match at a time.
Each one will have an individual board. So that, an asynchronous code will be required.
