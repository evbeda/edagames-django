About Code Challenge
Code Challenge is designed for the selection process of developers with junior profiles. We are convinced that playing is one of the best ways to learn, and also to demonstrate your knowledge and skills while having fun enjoying the process.
Documentation
All the information that you need to tackle this challenge you could find it in this documentation .
So, how you can play?
To participate it is necessary to have a bot created. You will make a code for your bot to play against the bots of the other participants. If you go to My Bots menu, you will see that you already have one created. This is your official bot whose name is your email username. Once you have your code written (which you can always improve), you can create challenges for other participants from Challenge menu and then check your results in My Matches menu.
